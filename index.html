Meet the Teachers

Veronica

¡Hola! I’m Veronica

I’ve taught Spanish to primary students for 8+ years. My classes are energetic and hands‑on — we sing, act, build, and most importantly use Spanish in real situations. I’m WWCC‑cleared and love collaborating with schools and families.

Specialties: beginners, pronunciation, games‑based learning.

Nancy

¡Hola! I’m Nancy

With a background in early childhood education, I focus on routines and gentle structure so kids feel safe to experiment with language. Expect crafts, movement breaks, and lots of positive feedback.

Specialties: early learners, inclusive classrooms, culture & crafts.
